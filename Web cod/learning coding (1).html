Things Royal Bengal Tiger do daily:

  1. Hunting and Feeding: Tigers spend a significant portion of their day hunting for prey, which includes deer, wild boar, and other large mammals. They rely on stealth and power to catch their food.
  2. Patrolling Territory: Tigers are territorial animals. They regularly patrol their territory to mark their boundaries with scent markings and to check for intruders.
  3. Resting and Sleeping: Tigers rest and sleep for several hours each day, often in shaded or secluded areas to conserve energy for hunting.
  4. Grooming: Tigers groom themselves by licking their fur, which helps to keep it clean, remove parasites, and maintain their health.
